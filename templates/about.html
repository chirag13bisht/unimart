About Us

UniMart is a project created by a group of students who understand the challenges and financial constraints faced by their peers. Our goal is to provide a platform where students can easily sell and rent out their used products, promoting sustainability and helping fellow students save money.

Our team is dedicated to improving the student experience by creating a reliable and easy-to-use platform that fosters connections and promotes a circular economy on campus.

Join us in building a vibrant and sustainable student community. Together, we can make a difference.
